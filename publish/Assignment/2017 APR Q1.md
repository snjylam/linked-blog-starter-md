**What are the risk of immediate annuities and the SCR modules?**
- Risk of policyholders live longer than expected - insurance risk module.
- Risk of expenses higher than expected - insurance risk module.
- Risk of asset liability mismatch, thus exposure to adverse movements in interest rates - market risk module.
- Risk of spreads widening on corporate bonds held - market risk module.
- Risk of defaults from reinsurance contracts or corporate bonds held - counterparty default risk module.
- Risk of failed internal process, people, and systems - operational risk module.
